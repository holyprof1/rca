Where Africa's fashion

Omiren Styles is RCA's sister editorial brand — an Africa-rooted fashion and culture platform covering the continent's designers, textile traditions, and aesthetic identity with the same authority RCA brings to travel and heritage.

The platform's scope extends beyond the continent. Omiren Styles covers Afro-Latino communities, the Caribbean, and the African diaspora worldwide, recognising that African aesthetic identity does not stop at any border.

The platform's ambition is to sit alongside the world's most authoritative fashion and culture publications in editorial standing, not as an Africa-interest supplement but as an equal voice in global fashion discourse, speaking from inside the continent and its diaspora rather than about them.

African Style Series — 40 articles across all regions documenting textile traditions from Maasai shuka to Ndebele beadwork to Itsekiri coral
The Omiren Argument — every article carries a clear editorial argument stateable in one sentence. Fashion as cultural identity, political assertion, and economic sovereignty
Shared audience — every RCA traveller interested in African culture is a natural Omiren Styles reader. Two platforms, one ecosystem
